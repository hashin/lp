Epilogue

This page has appeared on the internet in many forms over the years. In a lot of ways, it reflected how I looked at the world at that point in time. I brought this domain at a moment's passion - it felt like a cool thing to do. I thought that it was a passing fancy - I might forget to renew it and it would just go away like that. But it didn't.

Like many things in my life, it has assumed a life of its own. So I have decided to keep it as it is now. It might change again, at some point in time. But till then, it will live like this here.

It is an ode to what I have been. It is an ode to what I am right now. It doesn't change a lot of things about me, but I believe that it might help me appreciate who I am - a bit better as I write about things I have learnt and care about.
